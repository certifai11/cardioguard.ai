Understanding Risk Levels

High Risk

Risk probability ≥70%

A high-risk result indicates a significant probability of coronary heart disease. Consult with a healthcare professional as soon as possible.

Moderate Risk

Risk probability 50-69%

A moderate-risk result suggests concerning factors that may increase heart disease risk. Discuss these results with your healthcare provider.

Low Risk

Risk probability <50%

A low-risk result suggests current risk factors do not strongly indicate heart disease. Maintain heart-healthy habits and regular check-ups.
